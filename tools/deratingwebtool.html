Quick guide
T_c_max: bare Cu busbar = 145 °C • terminal = 100 °C
ΔT_c_self: component self-heat above local internal air (from test or calc)
ΔT_i_ref: auto-derived = T_c_max − T_amb_ref − ΔT_c_self (panel at limit)
A_eff: weighted enclosure surface (IEC 60890 §2.3) — only affects result when fans used
r_ref / r_new: 0.10–0.20 typical indoor NC; 0 = pure convection
Q_ref: 0 if reference was natural convection (most common)
