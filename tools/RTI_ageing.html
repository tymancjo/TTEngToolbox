Safety Considerations

  • Mechanical Capture: If properties drop below 50%, treat material as brittle. Support pieces mechanically.
  • Thermal Stress: Daily temperature swings cause expansion. Ensure gaps exist to prevent fatigue cracking.
  • Visual Inspection: Discoloration or "crazing" indicates the end of chemical life is approaching.
